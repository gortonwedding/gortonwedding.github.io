Countdown to Wedding

Alicia & Acton

Acton and Alicia are currently students at the University of Illinois. Alicia is a licenced nurse, and is working on her BA. Acton is a PhD canadidate, hoping to finish his work in the next coming year.

How we Met

Acton likes to emphasize, we went on Craigslist

Technically, that's true... but it wasn't as scandalous as that sounds.

It was the ROOMMATE section on CraigsList...

We just lucked out.

Sometime in February of 2010, Acton was searching for a new roommate while Alicia was moving home from Aurora.

We were both still playing the dating scene, but as time passed on, our friendship grew as we found ourselves enjoying each other's company more and more.

Against our better judgement, we took a chance.

We fell in love.

The Proposal

Our fifth anniversary was coming up on New Years Eve, we wanted to do something special. After searching a few locations, we decided on Chicago, and found an event called Chi-town Rising. At first we were apprehensive but quickly found out Acton’s friend Boyang was playing at the event, we decided to go.

The evening was brisk but beautiful. We spent the evening together, celebrating us. At midnight, fireworks began to go off all around us. We decided to get closer to the Chicago river, and under the night sky, with snow falling, and fireworks going off, Acton proposed. He of course put his spin on it, using augmented reality, over-laid “Will you Marry me” on a picture.
